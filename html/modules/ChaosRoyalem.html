Chaos Royale

Adobe Animate & Adobe Premiere

Chaos Royale is an animation developed in Adobe Animate, and serves as a visual narrative to the song "Chaos Royale" by Sister Sin. The music video depicts a confrontation between two adversaries within a coliseum, the sinister Black Knight is pitted against the valiant Hero of the coliseum.

As the combatants converge at the heart of the arena, the tide of battle takes an unexpected turn. When the Hero gains the upper hand, the Black Knight breaks out of their restricting armor to unveil she is a barbarian woman. The ensuing skirmish unfolds with a visceral intensity, leaving the audience in a state of both disgust and shock.
