Operation HAWK

Xcode & Swift

Developed on the XCode platform specifically for iOS devices, this therapeutic scheduling app is designed with this really specialized, vital focus on helping students with autism navigate their class schedules more efficiently. The application essentially streamlines the entire scheduling process, which allows therapists to create specific entries for students by inputting the exact time and appointment details. It’s about taking that logistical friction and making it manageable.

Now, the truly distinctive feature here unfolds in the augmented reality section. In this mode, therapists can set up a visual guide for students using a series of strategically placed AR rings. These rings serve as a navigational aid, guiding the student to their designated destination within the educational environment. Because the therapist is the one creating this visual guide, it ensures the experience is tailored and supportive for each individual student’s needs.

Once that schedule is configured and the AR guide is in place, the student engages with the app on their tablet. Using this wonderfully interactive approach, the child uses a virtual helicopter interface to navigate through those augmented reality rings, effectively steering themselves toward their destination. It’s this dynamic and engaging process that offers students a way to independently traverse their daily schedules, which is just a fascinating way to use technology for real-world empowerment.
