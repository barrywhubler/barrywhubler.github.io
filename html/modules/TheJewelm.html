The Jewel

Adobe Premiere

"The Jewel" is an immersive 360-degree VR experience that drops you right into this mischievous world of pranksters whose latest stunt goes—well, let’s just say perilously wrong thanks to a wizard’s cursed jewel. We used this dynamic fusion of Unity and Adobe Premiere as our creative canvas, which allowed us to build something that feels both technologically advanced and just really visually compelling.

I was deep in the weeds on this one—I helped write the script, and I actually voiced both the Old Man and Chuck. Plus, I handled the meticulous editing needed to make sure the final product looked polished and professional. It’s the kind of project that really pushes past the conventional boundaries of video to offer something much more immersive.
