F-117 Nighthawk

Blender

This F-117 Nighthawk stealth bomber was made in Blender. It was designed for a project that integrated machine learning algorithms for autonomous flight and precision bombing of designated targets. The primary goal was to implement cutting-edge technology that enabled the jet to navigate independently and execute targeted bombing runs.

The model has articulated flaps on both the wings and tail, enhancing its visual realism and contributing to its interactive nature.
