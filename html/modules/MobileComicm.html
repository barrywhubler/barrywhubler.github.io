Loopus

Adobe Animate

"Loopus" is a mobile comic developed as part of a group assignment utilizing Adobe Animate. This interactive narrative unfolds as a story revolving around a band of seasoned monster hunters, a temporal disturbance, and a great beast that demands an even greater sacrifice.

The storyline delves into themes of regret, exploring the complexities surrounding decision-making when the greater good demands significant personal sacrifices.
