The Jewel

Adobe Premiere

"The Jewel" exists as an immersive 360-degree or a Virtual Reality video. This narrative unfolds within the mischievous world of pranksters whose antics take a perilous turn involving a wizard's cursed jewel. The dynamic fusion of Unity and Adobe Premiere serves as the creative canvas for this project, offering a technologically advanced and visually compelling experience.

I contributed to the scripting, and I also assumed the roles of The Old Man and Chuck. Additionally, my responsibilities extended to the meticulous editing required to achieve a polished and visually appealing final product.This project transcends the conventional boundaries of video production, offering viewers a more immersive experience.
